📚 Online Course on Equitable Digital Access

Duration: 30 Hours

🎯 Course Outcomes

  • ✅ Analyze the evolution of disability models to determine their impact on societal attitudes.
  • ✅ Evaluate the effectiveness of contemporary models in addressing the needs of persons with disabilities.
  • ✅ Design inclusive communication strategies that align with universal accessibility principles.
  • ✅ Compare various assistive technologies and their applicability to specific disability types.
  • ✅ Develop comprehensive solutions for individuals with multiple disabilities using assistive technologies.
