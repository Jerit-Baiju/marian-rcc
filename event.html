Panel Discussion

Marian College Kuttikkanam Autonomous

Panel Discussion Details

A panel discussion on “Shaping Perspectives: Disability Narratives, Societal Perceptions, and Accessible Design” will be held on 8th October of 2024 at Marian College Kuttikkanam Autonomous conducted by first year BCA students in collaboration with Responsible Computing Challenge (RCC), Mozilla. The panellists are Dr. Joshin Joseph Assistant Professor, Department of Commerce, Marian College Kuttikanam, Mr. Kurian E J, Head Master, Gov. Higher Secondary School for the Visual Impairment, Olessa Kottayam, Mr. Amal George MSW student LISSAH, Calicut, Ms. Kavya, Assistant Professor, Department of Hindi, Prajyoti Niketan College, Thrissur and a moderator.

The panel discussion examined the intersection of disability narratives and accessible design. In the first hour, panellists shared personal experiences and insights into how societal perceptions, cultural representations, advocacy efforts, and persistent stereotypes influence social interactions, employment opportunities, and public policy for individuals with disabilities. The second hour focused on accessible design, highlighting its critical role in ensuring digital spaces are inclusive and equitable, addressing diverse needs, and fostering autonomy and equal participation in the digital landscape.
